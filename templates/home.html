Plan It

Your Day Your Way allows you store all your favourite workout and recipes in one easy place. You can see what other users add and even rate all recipes and workouts to let others know what you would recommend. Others can rate yours too! By being able to view all of your content on your profile page you can easily plan what you want to eat and how you want to work out that day….any day…it’s your day.

Eat It

If there are recipes that you have to repeatedly search to find you can add the link and you will be able to easily find them from your profile anytime you want. Decide you don’t like a recipe anymore, no problem, just delete it. Get creative and change a few ingredients, no problem, you can update it! You can even share photos of your beautiful creations.

Work It

Wish you could do the same with workouts as you can with recipes…well you can! Add your own workouts and get some inspiration from workouts other users upload. If you have a session at the gym that you want to keep and share it with others you can. Sometimes there are workouts online we love, you can save the link in you profile and come back to it anytime. You're in control!
